About

These character classes are formatted in the Old-School Essentials style with parchment backgrounds and clear, readable tables. All classes use the To-Hit bonus system for easier gameplay.

Each class page includes complete information on requirements, prime requisites, hit dice, special abilities, and full level progression tables with saving throws and class-specific features.
